What's in the kit

Marg Rakshak is a 2-component retrofit kit — designed to provide continuous side monitoring and clear in-cab alerts.

Side mirror sensors
High-precision multi-range sensors mount near the side mirrors and monitor the side & blind zones for approaching vehicles and obstacles.
Central dashboard screen
A compact in-cab display shows live proximity, relative motion, and issues audio-visual alerts when risk thresholds are crossed.

How it works

Sense
Side sensors continuously scan for vehicles and obstacles.
Track
Tracks relative position & speed of detected objects.
Predict
On-device model predicts collision risk using trajectory & speed.
Alert
Visual + audible alerts on the dash to warn the driver immediately.
Kit contents
  • 2 × Side mirror sensors

  • Mounts & basic harness

Frequently Asked Questions

Will Marg Rakshak work on my old car?
Yes. Sensors mount near side mirrors; the central unit runs on 12V power and is built to integrate with most vehicle types.
How long is the warranty?
Standard warranty: 6–12 months depending on purchase & installation option.
Is installation difficult?
Installation is straightforward for a trained technician — sensors are mirror-mounted and the compact central unit installs inside the cabin. We provide guides and can arrange local installers in many cities.
Will I get false alarms in slow traffic?
We use adaptive sensitivity to reduce alerts in slow/stop traffic and increase sensitivity at higher speeds to minimize false alarms.
Can Marg Rakshak be used in fleet vehicles?
Yes — fleet packages and optional analytics are available to help fleet managers monitor safety events.
Does the system need internet?
No — core detection and alerting work offline on-device. Optional analytics or cloud services require connectivity and consent.
