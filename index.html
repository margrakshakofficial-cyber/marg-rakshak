Marg Rakshak Smart Surround-Aware Safety System

A compact retrofit kit providing continuous side monitoring for cars and three-wheelers. The system combines two core components to sense the surroundings and inform the driver in real time.

Side mirror sensors — High-precision dual sensors that mount near each side mirror to provide near and mid-range detection of vehicles, bikes and obstacles approaching from the sides.
Central dashboard screen (safety box) — The central processing unit that aggregates sensor data, runs risk models, and displays visual alerts plus audible warnings to the driver.

How it works

1. Sense
Sensors continuously monitor side zones and detect moving objects.
2. Fuse
Central unit combines data from both sensors to build a live view of surrounding objects.
3. Predict
On-device risk models calculate trajectories and closure rates to assess threat level.
4. Alert
When risk exceeds thresholds, visual and audible alerts are issued on the dashboard screen.
What's in the kit
  • Side mirror sensors (x2)
  • Central dashboard safety box (x1)

Frequently Asked Questions

Will Marg Rakshak work on my old car?
Yes. Marg Rakshak is designed for retrofit on a wide range of vehicles including older cars and three-wheelers. Sensors mount near the side mirrors and the compact central box connects to the vehicle's 12V power supply for operation.
How long is the warranty?
Our standard warranty is 6–12 months, depending on the purchase channel and installation option. Warranty terms are provided at the time of purchase.
Is installation difficult?
Installation is straightforward for a trained installer: sensors are mounted near the side mirrors, the central safety box is placed inside the cabin, and a single 12V power connection is used. We provide a step-by-step guide and can arrange local installation support in many cities.
Will I get false alarms in slow traffic?
The system uses adaptive sensitivity to reduce alerts in slow or congested traffic while increasing sensitivity at higher speeds. This reduces unnecessary alarms in city driving.
Can I use Marg Rakshak for fleet vehicles?
Yes — we offer fleet deployment support and optional analytics packages for fleet managers to monitor events and improve driver safety across a fleet.
What happens if the sensors are tampered with?
Tamper detection triggers an alert and logs the event in the system. You can view tamper logs or contact support for assistance and investigation.
Does Marg Rakshak need internet to work?
No — core detection, risk modeling and alerting happen on-device for low latency. Optional cloud features (analytics or remote monitoring) require connectivity and explicit consent.
